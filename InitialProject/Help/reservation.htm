Reservation

This is a tour reservation form. Simply enter the average age of all guests you want to reserve for, as well as the number of seats for the reservation. If you have won any vouchers, you can activate them in the form below, keeping in mind that they will be removed from your voucher list afterwards. By clicking on RESERVE, you confirm your reservation, and it will be forwarded to the tour guides to add you to their list of guests. If you change your mind and are unsure about making the reservation, you can cancel by clicking the CANCEL button.
