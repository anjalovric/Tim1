Vouchers

Here you can see all the vouchers you have earned and their expiration dates. There are three groups of vouchers: 1) Vouchers obtained due to tour cancellations, which are valid for one year.
2) Vouchers earned for participating in at least five tours within a year, which are valid for six months.
3) Vouchers obtained for reporting guide cancellations, which have the longest validity period of two years.
